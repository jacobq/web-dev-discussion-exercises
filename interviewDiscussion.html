Ameriprise Front-end Developer Discussion

Introduction/Basics

  • Tell us about your most recent work.
  • Do you have anything you want to show us?

...move on to social/technical questions...

  • Before we walk around/finish, are there qsuestions you have for us?

Social/Communications/Work Management

  • Workflow/Social/Mgt

    • What sort of workflow do you use/like?
    • Are you familiar with Agile? What does Agile mean to you?
    • When faced with a challenge, when do you ask for help?
    • What do you look for in a workplace? Try to avoid?

  • Tools/Resources

    • What are your favorite tools for editing code?
    • What are your favorite tools for debugging/testing?
    • What are some of your favorite resources?
    • Are you familiar with Git or other VCS?
    • Do you have experience with working the CLI?
    • Are you familiar with any build or automation tools? (node.js, grunt, etc)

Technical

  • General

    • What is Semantic?
    • What is "good code" to you? What types of things do you look for during a code review?
    • Of the three main disciplines in front-end development, what would you say is your strongest (HTML, CSS, JS)?

  • HTML

    • What are ways of creating lists in HTML?
